Tacos

A taco is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a small hand-sized corn- or wheat-based tortilla topped with a filling. The tortilla is then folded around the filling and eaten by hand.

Ingredients:

    Tortillas:

  • Corn or flour tortillas.

    • Protein:

  • Ground beef, chicken, pork, or plant-based alternatives.

    • Toppings:

  • Diced onion, chopped cilantro.
  • Salsa (spicy, mild, or verde).
  • Lime wedges.
  • Guacamole or sliced avocado.
  • Shredded cheese or sour cream (optional)
  1. Prepare the protein:

    2. Cook ground beef, chicken, or pork in a pan with oil. Season with cumin, paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper.

  2. Heat the tortillas:

    3. Warm them in a pan or directly over a flame for a slight char.

  3. Assemble:

    4. Add the protein to the tortilla and top with onion, cilantro, salsa, and a squeeze of lime. Add guacamole or shredded cheese as desired.
