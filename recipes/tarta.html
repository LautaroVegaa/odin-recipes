Tarta

A tart is a baked dish consisting of a filling over a pastry base with an open top not covered with pastry. The pastry is usually shortcrust pastry; the filling may be sweet or savoury, though modern tarts are usually fruit-based, sometimes with custard.

Ingredients:

    Base:

  • Pastry crust or pre-made pie dough.

    • Filling (common combinations):

  • Spinach and ricotta cheese.
  • Ham and cheese.
  • Pumpkin and mozzarella.
  • Eggs (to mix with the filling).
  • Cream or milk (for a smooth texture).
  • Salt, pepper, nutmeg (optional).
  1. Prepare the crust:

    2. Line a pie dish with pre-made pastry or homemade dough.

  2. Prepare the filling:

    3. Combine the ingredients in a bowl and mix well.

  3. For spinach:

    4. Sauté spinach in a pan with garlic, then mix with ricotta cheese, eggs, salt, pepper, and nutmeg.

  4. For ham and cheese:

    5. Layer ham and cheese slices with a mixture of eggs and milk for creaminess.

  5. Assemble:

    6. Pour the filling into the crust. Optionally, cover with another pastry layer and seal the edges.

  6. Cook:

    7. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30–40 minutes until golden.
